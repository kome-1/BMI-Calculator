NHS an expert in BMI

B.M.I is an acronym for Body Mass Index which is a measurement of a person's leanness or corpulence based on their height and weight, and is intended to quantify tissue mass.
It is an indicator of whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height.
The BMI value is then used to categorize if a person is underweight, normal weight, over weight, or obese depending on what range the value falls between.
